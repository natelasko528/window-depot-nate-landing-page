Connect Facebook & Instagram

Get real per-post engagement data directly from Meta's Graph API.

Why Connect Meta Directly?

GoHighLevel gives us aggregate 7-day stats, but no per-post engagement. By connecting the Meta Graph API directly, we get per-post impressions, reach, likes, comments, shares, saves, and clicks for every Facebook and Instagram post.

Setup Steps

1

Create a Meta App

Go to developers.facebook.com/apps → "Create App" → choose "Other" use case → "Business" type → name it anything (e.g. "WD Dashboard").

2

Add Facebook Login Product

In your app dashboard, click "Add Product" → find "Facebook Login for Business" → click "Set Up". Under Settings → Valid OAuth Redirect URIs, add:

Loading...
3

Copy App ID & Secret

In your app's Settings → Basic, copy the App ID and App Secret. Add them as environment variables in Vercel:

META_APP_ID=your_app_id_here
META_APP_SECRET=your_app_secret_here

Then redeploy the project.

4

Authorize the App

Click the button below to authorize. You'll be redirected to Facebook to grant these permissions:

  • pages_read_engagement — Read post engagement metrics
  • pages_show_list — List your Facebook Pages
  • read_insights — Read Page and post analytics
  • instagram_basic — Access Instagram account info
  • instagram_manage_insights — Read Instagram post insights
Connect with Facebook

You'll be redirected to Facebook to approve access.

Privacy: This connection is between your own Meta App and your own Facebook/Instagram accounts. No data is shared with third parties. Tokens are stored only in your Vercel environment variables.

After Authorization

After you authorize, you'll see your Page Access Tokens. Copy them and add these final env vars in Vercel:

META_PAGE_TOKEN=your_page_token
META_PAGE_ID=your_page_id
META_IG_USER_ID=your_instagram_id (if applicable)

Page tokens obtained this way never expire. Your dashboard will then show real per-post engagement data.
